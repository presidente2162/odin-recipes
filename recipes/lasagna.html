Description

Oh-my-lasagna! Have you ever craved the cheesy deliciousness of

of a hot, steaming lasagna? If you answered yes, there is only one solution: Lasagna.

So, sit back, relax and enjoy the following lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasgana pasta noodles
  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Big Juicey Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Shallots

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Crush tomatoes and add them to the marinara sauce
  3. Add olive oil to pan
  4. On medium high heat, sautee shallots and garlic
  5. When fragarent, add mushrooms
  6. Cook mushrooms until tender, then add to marinara sauce
  7. Butter bottom of a cassarole dish
  8. Add layer of lasagna noodles in the buttered cassarole dish
  9. Layer upgraded marinara sauce ontop of noodles
  10. Repeat steps 8-9 until cassarole dish is full
  11. Cook lasagna in oven for 35-45 minutes

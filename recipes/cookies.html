Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Plant Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Caramel
  • Dried Cranberry
  • Baking Soda
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix all ingredients into a bowl
  3. Make little balls from the bowl of ingredients
  4. Spread balls on tinfoil lined cooking sheet
  5. Bake for 15 minutes

Description

Might Morphin' Power Ranges! Green rangers, let's tell those bad guys to

take a hike! Hold up, first we need some Pizzaaaaa

Our little turtle helpers have been up all night trying to develop the juicest pizza

for your tounges! You will not regret taking a slice out of this pie, baby. Morphs up, DUUDDEEE!!!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara
  • Four cheese blend
  • Parmasean
  • Basil
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Garlic

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 415°F
  2. Spin pizza dough until 1/3in thick with an approximately 4in radius
  3. Spread thin layer of marinara over one side of pizza dough
  4. Sprinkle desired amount of garlic on pizza
  5. Coat that sum-a-beach in a butt load of four cheese blend
  6. Shave some of that sweeeeet parmaseano on that pizza
  7. Slice, quarter, pat dry fresh tomatoes and sparingly add to pizza
  8. Place pizza in oven for 15 minutes
  9. Finger shred basil and sprinkle over the pizza! Belisimo.
